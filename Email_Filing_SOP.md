# SOP: Email Filing in HubSpot

**Prepared by:** Jessa Mae Lizaso

**Objective:**  
Ensure all emails are organized in HubSpot for client follow-up and tracking.

**Steps:**  
1. Check inbox for client and team emails.  
2. Tag emails with client name and deal ID.  
3. Upload attachments to the corresponding HubSpot client folder.  
4. Update the task tracker to mark email as "Filed".  
5. Highlight urgent emails for manager review.  

**Tips:**  
- Double-check client info before filing.  
- Maintain confidentiality at all times.  
- Complete filing daily to avoid backlog.
